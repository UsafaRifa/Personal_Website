About Me

I am Usafa Akther Rifa from Dhaka, Bangladesh. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology, where I developed strong skills in C/C++, Java, and Python, and gained expertise in database management and machine learning.

I've worked on projects such as a mobile app for promoting a healthy lifestyle and a desktop game with engaging gameplay. Currently, I teach coding at Dreamers Academy, where I enjoy mentoring young coders.

My research focuses on machine learning, particularly computer vision and natural language processing (NLP). I’ve worked on projects involving deep learning for personalized diet recommendations, fueling my enthusiasm for applying AI to real-world problems.

As I advance in my career, I remain committed to contributing to both education and research. My journey is marked by a dedication to continuous learning and professional growth, and I am excited about the opportunities and challenges that lie ahead.

Thank you for learning about my background. I look forward to exploring new advancements and achieving future successes.
